What is Eramen?

About Us

Eramen was first founded in 1910 in Tokyo, Japan by a ramen chef named Akihiro Yamashita. All ramen menus at Eramen are made from the best quality organic flour which produces a delicious taste. Over time Eramen began to open branches in various countries in Asia, Europe and America. In 2015 Eramen opened its first branch in Jakarta, Indonesia.

History of ERamen Restaurant

1910

First Store

Founded in the city of Tokyo, Japan by a famous ramen chef at that time named Akihiro Yamashita.

1915

Opening First Branch

The first Eramen branch opened in Fukuoka, Japan because the main Eramen store in Tokyo was very busy with customers.

1930

Opened several branches in Japan

The success of Eramen's flagship store in Tokyo has enabled Eramen to open several new branches in Kobe, Nagoya and Okinawa.

1950

Closed several branches

The emergence of several new ramen restaurants offering lower prices has forced several Eramen branches to close.

1965

The glory days are back

After suffering heavy losses over the past few years, Eramen was finally able to return to its former glory.

1990

Expanding to several countries

The success achieved by Eramen in Japan has made Eramen successful in opening branches in several countries in Asia, Europe, and America.

2015

The first store in Indonesia

Eramen opened its first outlet in Jakarta and received high enthusiasm from the public.

2023

Several branches in Indonesia

Currently Eramen has opened several branches in Indonesia spread across the cities of Jakarta, Bandung and Malang.
