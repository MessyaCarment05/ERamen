About the company Eramen Restaurant Eramen was first founded in 1910 in Tokyo, Japan by a ramen chef named Akihiro Yamashita. All ramen menus at Eramen are made from the best quality organic flour which produces a delicious taste. Over time Eramen began to open branches in various countries in Asia, Europe and America. In 2015 Eramen opened its first branch in Jakarta, Indonesia.
